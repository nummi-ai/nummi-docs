## What makes Nummi special?

Nummi combines the power of advanced AI models (Claude 4 Sonnet, GPT-4) with practical tools that actually make your work easier:

### 🧠 **Smart AI That Remembers**
- **Lifetime Memory**: Your AI assistant remembers your conversations, goals, and work context
- **Smart Model Routing**: Automatically selects the best AI model for each task
- **Custom Personas**: Create AI assistants tailored to your specific needs

### 📧 **Email Made Effortless**
- **AI Summaries**: Get intelligent summaries of every email
- **Smart Replies**: Generate contextual responses in seconds
- **Batch Processing**: Handle multiple emails with one click

### 🎨 **Content Creation Powerhouse**
- **Multi-Platform Publishing**: Create once, publish everywhere (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram)
- **Smart Scheduling**: AI suggests optimal posting times
- **Blog Studio**: Full-featured writing environment with SEO optimization
